The founders

The legacy

Chef Vineet Bhatia MBE

He won Indian food its first ever Michelin star. Then he kept going.

Pioneer of Indian Progressive Cuisine. Restaurants around the world with Harrods, Marriott and The Oberoi. Two cookbooks, a judge’s seat on Netflix’s The Final Table and MasterChef India, and a GREAT Campaign ambassadorship for Britain. Decades at the very top of fine dining, now poured into the world’s most underrated bread.

  • First Michelin star for Indian cuisine
  • The Final Table · MasterChef India
  • Harrods · Marriott · The Oberoi
@chefvineet
